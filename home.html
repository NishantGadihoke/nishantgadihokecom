Nishant Gadihoke is a multi-disciplinary designer from New Delhi, India.

About

Hi! I'm Nishant. I’m a highschool senior at The Mother’s International School in New Delhi. I work with organisations and individuals to create intuitive and efficient solutions ranging from ecommerce branding to web browser development.

With over 3 years of experience in the field, working with 10+ startups as a consultant, I have an eye for finding elegant ways to solve complex problems. I have worked with Google, KhanAcademy, and The WikiMedia Foundation in the past.

I’m currently running my own startup, Oswald Labs, which creates accessibility technology for the differently abled. I’ve been invited to talk at multiple events about design, development, and accessibility technology; I also hosted North India’s largest hackathon earlier this year.

Work

I brainstorm design on whiteboards, in notebooks, on palms, or whatever I can find. Then, I bring it onto a screen using PhotoShop or Sketch and code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
